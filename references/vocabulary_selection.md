# Vocabulary Selection

## Quantity
- 15-25 items for 8-25 min videos
- 8-12 items for short videos
- Up to 30 for vocabulary-focused videos

## Prioritize
- Explicitly taught items
- Repeated or emphasized words
- Phrasal verbs and idioms
- Collocations (e.g., "make a decision")
- Sentence frames (e.g., "I was wondering if...")
- Discourse markers (e.g., "actually", "basically")

## Avoid
- Rare but irrelevant words
- Proper nouns, brand names
- Basic function words
- Isolated words without context

## Ranking (when too many candidates)
1. Teaching emphasis
2. Reusability
3. Frequency
4. Nuance value
5. Connection to video goal
